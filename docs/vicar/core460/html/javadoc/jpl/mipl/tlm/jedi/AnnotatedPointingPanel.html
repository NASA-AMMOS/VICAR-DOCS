extends java.awt.Panel
implements java.awt.event.MouseListener, java.awt.event.MouseMotionListener
    A JEDI widget that graphically represents the pointing geometry of the science instrument. Typically, this geometry is for a landed instrument in azimuth/elevation coordinated, though it could be extended for other uses.

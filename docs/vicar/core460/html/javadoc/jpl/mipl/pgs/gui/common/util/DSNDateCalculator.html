      • weeksInYear

        public static int weeksInYear(int year)
                       throws java.text.ParseException
        Returns the number of DSN weeks in the given year 
          DSN week starts on monday, must contain at least 4 days
  in a particular year to be considered part of that year

  365 days/year except leap year is 366

  365 days = 52 wks + 1 day

  In a normal year the year ends on the same
  day-of-week as it starts

  In a leap year the year ends on the day after
  the day-of-week that it starts

  So, in a normal year, if Jan 1 falls on:

   Mon    first week 7 days in the same year: Mon through Sun
          final week 1 day in the same year:  Mon, Dec 31

   Tues   first week 6 days: Tues through Sun
          final week 2 days:  Mon & Tues

   Wed    first week 5 days: Wed through Sun
          final week 3 days:  Mon through Wed

   Thurs  first week 4 days: Thurs through Sun
          final week 4 days:  Mon through Thurs

   Fri    first week 3 days: Fri through Sun
          final week 5 days:  Mon through Fri

   Sat    first week 2 days: Sat & Sun
          final week 6 days:  Mon through Sat

   Sun    first week 1 day:  Sun
          final week 7 days:  Mon through Sun

  In a leap year, if Jan 1 falls on:

   Mon    first week 7 days in the same year: Mon through Sun
          final week 2 days in the same year:  Mon & Tues, Dec 31

   Tues   first week 6 days: Tues through Sun
          final week 3 days:  Mon through Wed

   Wed    first week 5 days: Wed through Sun
          final week 4 days:  Mon through Thurs

   Thurs  first week 4 days: Thurs through Sun
          final week 5 days:  Mon through Fri

   Fri    first week 3 days: Fri through Sun
          final week 6 days:  Mon through Sat

   Sat    first week 2 days: Sat & Sun
          final week 7 days:  Mon through Sun, Dec 31

   Sun    first week 1 day:  Sun
          final week 7 days:  Mon through Sun, Dec 30
          (Dec 31 is the following Monday)
        Parameters:
        year - The year in yyyy format
        Returns:
        The number of DSN weeks in the year (ranging from 52 to 53)
        Throws:
        java.text.ParseException - Signals that an error has been reached unexpectedly while parsing.

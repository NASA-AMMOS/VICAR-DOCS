Help for TCLMATH

purpose
	A routine to perform math operations on tcl variables.

	AVERAGE, SQROOT, SQUARE, CUBE, NATLOG, LOG, and  EXP

    LN and NATLOG are the same
    LOG and LOG10 are the same

Help for CCOMP

PURPOSE

    CCOMP converts images between complex pixel format and two real
format images.  Two types of transformation are possible (amplitude and phase)
or (real and imaginary).  The transformation may be done in either direction:
a complex image to two real images, or two real images to a complex image.


EXECUTION

    ccomp  IN.CMP  (OUT.AMP,OUT.PH)
    ccomp  IN.CMP  (OUT.RE, OUT.IM)  'RECT

    ccomp  (IN.AMP,IN.PH)  OUT.CMP    'POLAR 'INVERSE
    ccomp  (IN.RE, IN.IM)  OUT.CMP    'RECT  'INVERSE

'POLAR is the default transformation and 'FORWARD is the default direction.

PARAMETERS:

INP

 For FORWARD mode: complex image For INVERSE mode: (real and imaginary) or (amplitude and phase) images

OUT

 For FORWARD mode: (real and imaginary) or (amplitude and phase) images For INVERSE mode: complex image

SIZE

 VICAR size field

SL

 Starting line

SS

 Starting sample

NL

 Number of lines

NS

 Number of samples

TRANS

 Keyword for the transformation: 'POLAR for amplitude and phase. 'RECTANG for real and imaginary.

DIRECT

 Keyword for the direction: FORWARD for complex input. INVERSE for complex output. .END
